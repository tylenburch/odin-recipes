Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian-American dish that features breaded and fried chicken cutlets topped with marinara sauce and melted cheese. It's a comforting and flavorful meal that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying
