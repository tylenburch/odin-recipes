Yellow Curry

Description

Yellow Curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish that originates from Thailand. It features a rich and creamy sauce made with coconut milk, yellow curry paste, and a variety of spices. The curry is typically cooked with vegetables and your choice of protein, such as chicken, shrimp, or tofu.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons yellow curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, onions)
  • 1 cup protein (e.g., chicken, shrimp, tofu)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the yellow curry paste. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Pour in the coconut milk and stir to combine with the curry paste. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Add the vegetables and protein to the skillet. Cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the protein is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  4. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Yellow Curry!
